The Real Cost of No Web Page

Every Day Without a Web Page
Is a Day Working Against You

Over 74% of the world is online, and your next client is already searching.
Here's what happens when they don't find you.

01

You're losing warm leads every day

Someone hears about your business and searches online. They find nothing. Or worse, a competitor with a proper page. That lead is gone before you even knew they existed.

02

You look less serious than you are

Sending prospects to your Instagram or a WhatsApp number signals that you're not yet established. It costs you trust before the conversation even starts. No matter how good your product is.

03

You're leaving real money on the table

Without a page working 24/7, you can only sell to people who already know you. Every missed search impression is missed revenue, and it compounds quietly every single week.

04

Your time goes to the wrong things

Explaining your services in DMs. Answering the same questions over and over. Sending voice notes with your price list. A proper web page handles all of this, so you can focus on the work you actually do.
